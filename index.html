Fatal Frame Characters


Images Description Trivia
Yuri Kozukata is the main protagonist of Fatal Frame: Maiden of Black Water. She has the ability to see and interact with spirits using "shadow reading" and the Camera Obscura. Haunted by her tragic past, Yuri explores the cursed Mount Hikami to find missing people and battle malevolent ghosts, while facing her own struggles with grief and loneliness. According to Fatal Frame: Maiden of Black Water's memo, Yuri's hobby is cycling.
Miu Hinasaki is another main character of Fatal Frame: Maiden of Black Water. She is the daughter of Miku Hinasaki, the protagonist of the original Fatal Frame. Miu goes on a journey to Mount Hikami to search for her missing mother. Like her mother, Miu can use the Camera Obscura to fight spirits. She struggles with feelings of abandonment, having known her father and feeling disconnected from her mother, which drives her desire to understand her origins. Miu is the first playable character of the game until being captured by a ghost.
Mayu and Mio Amakura are twin sisters and protagonists of Fatal Frame II: Crimson Butterfly. The game follows the sisters as they become trapped in the abandoned Minakami Village, a place cursed by a failed ritual involving twins. The twins' bond is central to the story, and their relationship mirrors the tragic fate of past twin sacrifices in the village, forcing them to confront a choice between life death, and loyalty. The main theme of Fatal Frame II is the Crimson Butterfly. The bow on the twins' outfits is shaped like a red butterfly.
